About Us

SKIN1004 is a South Korean skincare brand known for its focus on natural and hypoallergenic products, particularly those containing Centella Asiatica extract sourced from Madagascar. The brand's name, pronounced "Skin-Angel" reflects the idea of skin-soothing care. SKIN1004 emphasizes clean, minimal formulations and avoids unnecessary additives, prioritizing the purity of Centella Asiatica.
